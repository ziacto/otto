DROP YOUR DIAGRAM IMAGES HERE
=============================

Files placed in this folder appear automatically in the app under:
  Knowledge Base → Diagrams tab

Supported formats: .png, .jpg, .jpeg, .webp

Suggested diagrams for a BMW 730li 2007 E65 GCC spec:

  - engine_bay_N52B30.png
  - fuse_box_glovebox.jpg
  - fuse_box_boot.jpg
  - fuse_box_engine_bay.jpg
  - vacuum_routing_N52.png
  - wiring_audio_system.png
  - wiring_dme_main.png
  - cooling_system_layout.png
  - serpentine_belt_routing.png

After adding files, rebuild the app:
  ./gradlew assembleDebug

The viewer supports:
  - Pinch to zoom (up to 6x)
  - Drag to pan when zoomed
  - Double-tap to toggle fit / 2x
